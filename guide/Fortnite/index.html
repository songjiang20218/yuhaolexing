Enable 2FA for Fortnite
Sign into your Epic Games account, and visit your Account page at https://www.epicgames.com
Step 1: Find the 2FA settings first
① Account ② Passwords& Security ③ Authenticator App
Step 2: Enable Authentication App
Refer to the following to Enable Authentication app.
Step 3: Add account to Authenticator App
"Scan QR code" or "Enter manually", or you can take a sceenshot of QR code, and "Import from Photo" to add account to Authenticator App.
Step 4: Verify your Code
Return to Fortnite App and enter the Code to verify.
When you're all set up,you can safely use 2FA now.
